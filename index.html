Andrew Eckholm

Andrew Eckholm is a writer living in New York. He received a B.A. from the University of Chicago in 2016 and an M.F.A. from the New School in 2020. His stories are often set in New York City and his essays often explore the link between personal fantasy and artistic style. His work has been published by America's leading literary magazines. His dreams are vivid and frequent.

He is also an expert on the college admissions process, with previous students admitted to every top university in the US, Canada, and the UK, as well as a deeply experienced writing tutor for students and applicants at all levels. Rates are available upon request.

The Point Magazine: Saying I Love You

2020. A short work of memoir about the death of my grandmother.

I’ve been nervously pacing, and as I allow myself to breathe once again I discover that I am in my grandmother’s room. The bed missing, the room is a jumble of cardboard boxes and misaligned furniture, strewn in a half-circle around an empty core. I look through her things for a while, searching, perhaps, for a more capacious love.

— Grief · Apartments · Love
